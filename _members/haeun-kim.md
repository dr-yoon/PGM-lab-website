# Postdoctoral Researcher

*Dr. Kim* received her Ph.D. in Chemistry from Yonsei University in 2026 under the supervision of Prof. Duhee Bang.  
Her doctoral research focused on developing high-throughput DNA synthesis technologies, including phosphoramidite-based oligonucleotide synthesis, DNA microarray fabrication, and open-source DNA synthesizer platforms.

She joined our lab in July, 2026.  
Her research interests lie in clinical application of genomic technologies, with particular expertise in oligonucleotide synthesis, synthetic biology, laboratory automation, and sequencing-based assays.  
She is interested in developing scalable experimental platforms that enable high-throughput genomic analyses and advance precision medicine.
