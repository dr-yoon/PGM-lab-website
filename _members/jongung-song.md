# Research Assistant

**Jongung** joined our lab as a Research Assistant in 2026. He received his M.S. in Intelligent Electronics and Computer Engineering from Chonnam National University, where he worked under the supervision of Prof. Sunyong Yoo.
His research interests focus on the application of machine learning to omics data, including genomics and transcriptomics. He is particularly interested in translational bioinformatics and the integration of biological and clinical data to advance precision medicine.
